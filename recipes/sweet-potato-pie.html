Sweet Potato Pie Recipe

Sweet Potato Pie

Thie recipe was shared with me by a special friend in Atlanta, GA. It has long been a favorite,
and everyone who tastes it says it is the best they have ever had.

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato
  • half cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • half cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • half teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • half teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
