Turkey Soup Recipe

Turkey Soup

Thie is a family favorite and nobody knows how easy it is to make! I love that it's gluten-free!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups diced cooked turkey
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 potato, peeled and diced
  • quarter cup chopped celery
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parley
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • half teaspoon salt
  • half teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 cups turkey stock
